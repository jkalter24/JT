Who We Are & What We Do

JunTechPC was established in 2005 by Juny Engelhart, specialising in SEO, Web Design, Computer Services and Support. He has had a love of computers all of his life, with his first computer running Windows 95. Being technically minded he took his first PC apart to 'rebuild' it – it was back in the very early 2000's and he has never looked back.

Juny Engelhart has been involved with computers, both from a programming perspective and the technical side, with experience in PC assembling, final-tester, Quality control and engineering server line since 2002 where he worked for I.M.S. Acer/IBM (The Netherlands).

Over the years Juny Engelhart has continued to increase his knowledge by taking numerous technical based computer courses and also has a good knowledge of most popular software packages.
